Confesionario breve, en lengua mexicana y castellana

Confesionario breve, en lengua mexicana y castellana

Abbreviated script for priests administering the sacrament of confession to Indigenous people. It includes the priest's initial admonition, then questions they should ask before the confession, on the Ten Commandments, to each gender, to married persons, to local officials, and on the seven mortal sins, concluding with an admonition.| Collation formula: a¹⁰. Format: 8o. Height: 22 cm. Binding: Dutch in leather and paper. Illustrations: Woodcut on the title page of Christ in a press and vignettes. Type: Gothic.

Written by Alonso de Molina // Published in Mexico (country)| México (country) by Balli, Pedro in 1577 // Retrieved by Biblioteca Francisco Xavier Clavigero, Universidad Iberoamericana
